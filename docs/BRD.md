# Business Requirement Document (BRD)

## 1. Project Overview
The project aims to build a social media platform where users can upload videos and photos. 
Content will be automatically boosted and recommended based on engagement metrics such as likes, comments, shares, and watch time.

## 2. Business Objectives
- Build a platform similar to Instagram / TikTok
- Help creators reach more audience
- Boost content automatically based on engagement
- Generate revenue through ads and promotions

## 3. Target Users
- Content creators
- Social media influencers
- General users who want entertainment
- Brands who want to promote products

## 4. Key Business Features
- User registration and login
- Upload photos and videos
- Engagement system (likes, comments, shares)
- Algorithm-based content boosting
- Personalized feed
- Trending section

## 5. Revenue Model
- Advertisements
- Paid content boosting
- Brand promotions
- Affiliate marketing

## 6. Success Metrics
- Number of daily active users
- Average watch time
- Engagement rate
- Viral content growth
